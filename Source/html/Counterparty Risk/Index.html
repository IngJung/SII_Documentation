Loss-Given-Default

Delegated Acts 2015 - Article 196

Par 1
"The loss-given-default shall be net of the liabilities towards counterparties belonging to the single name exposure."
"No offsetting shall be allowed for if the liabilities are expected to be met before the credit exposure is cleared."
Liabilities, i.e. RI premium, can be netted off, but not if you expect to pay the premium before the RI pays back their share of the claims (this is the case with most RI).
